TimeToPrint

A smart self-service printing solution that lets users upload documents from their phone, pay online, and collect prints from an automated kiosk — no queues, no USB drives, no waiting on staff.

Problem statement01 / 09

Printing documents is often inconvenient — visiting a print shop, waiting for staff availability, transferring files manually, and standing in queues. During busy hours it gets worse, especially for students who need urgent printouts before classes or exams.

Objective

Design a seamless, self-serve printing experience: upload documents remotely, pay digitally, get real-time print status, and collect the finished job without ever talking to a shop operator.

User research03 / 09

Research surfaced consistent friction in how people print: long waits at shops, files passed around manually over WhatsApp or USB, limited payment options, no visibility into progress, and real difficulty getting anything printed during peak hours.

Key insights

  • Long waiting times at print shops
  • Manual file transfers through WhatsApp or USB drives
  • Limited payment options
  • No visibility into print progress
  • Difficulty printing documents during peak hours

Solution & features04 / 09

TimeToPrint offers a fully digital printing experience. Users upload a PDF, Word document, or image from their phone; choose paper size, color, copies, and orientation; pay securely online; and get a QR code that authenticates them at the kiosk. From there, they can track the job in real time and collect it the moment it's done.

Challenges & decisions08 / 09

Challenges

  • Simplifying the printing workflow into a few intuitive steps
  • Designing a responsive interface for both mobile and kiosk displays
  • Balancing functionality with a clean, uncluttered interface
  • Making the process understandable without any guidance

Design decisions

  • Minimal interface to reduce cognitive load
  • QR-code authentication instead of manual verification
  • Step-by-step workflow to minimize user errors
  • Large touch targets and readable type for kiosk interaction
  • Live print tracking for transparency and confidence
Outcome & learnings09 / 09

TimeToPrint streamlines the traditional printing experience by cutting wait times, removing manual file transfers, and enabling secure, contactless printing — improving efficiency while staying simple for students and professionals alike.

Key learnings

This project strengthened my user-centered design, workflow optimization, interaction design, and prototyping skills. It also sharpened my sense of designing for two very different surfaces — a mobile app and a physical kiosk — while keeping the experience consistent across both.
