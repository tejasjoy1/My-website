About

A designer who backs every decision with data.

I'm Tejas — an Information Science and Engineering student at DSATM, currently in my 7th semester. I work across UI/UX design, graphic design, and data analytics — starting with the user and the numbers, then designing interfaces and visuals that reflect what the data actually says.

That's taken me from designing and prototyping product interfaces, to building visual identities and layouts as a graphic designer, and most recently to interning as a Data Analytics Intern at Unlox Academy, in collaboration with Finsetisis. I care less about how a project looks in a demo and more about whether the decisions behind it hold up.
